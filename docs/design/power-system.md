# Power System — "Strata Resonance"

> Status: **Direction locked** (Phase 0). Mechanical details (numbers, ability
> kits, balancing) are deferred to Phase 1 (GDD) — this doc captures the
> *identity* the rest of the design builds on.

## The phenomenon

A character's power is generated by **feeling** — the emotional charge behind
a moment, not a measured force of will. That charge expresses itself as
**resonant vibration**: rippling shockwaves, crystalline growth patterns, and
harmonic distortion in the air around them. The vibration is the *visual cue*
the feeling produces, not the source of the power itself — power isn't
"burning" outward, it's **resonating** outward: building, amplifying, and
occasionally shattering things that can't withstand the frequency an emotion
reaches.

This replaces KHR's "Dying Will Flames" concept entirely — different sense
(sound/vibration vs. fire), different visual register (geometric ripples and
crystal growth vs. flame), different emotional metaphor (resonance/harmony vs.
burning passion).

## Attribute wheel — "Resonance States" × emotional registers

> Superseded the earlier geological-forces wheel (Magma/Crystal/Tectonic/
> Mineral/Erosion/Seismic/Pressure) — replaced with a five-type wheel where
> each type is both a *way energy resonates* and the *emotional register* it
> springs from. This double meaning is the signature of the system: a
> character's type isn't an arbitrary assignment, it's a reflection of their
> inner emotional shape — and the type matchups read as emotionally true
> rather than memorized trivia.

| Type | Resonance state | Emotional register | Beats... because |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Bedrock** | Dense, solid, immovable | Resolve, conviction, stubbornness, protectiveness | **Drift** — steadfastness grounds a wandering, unfocused mind |
| **Drift** | Scattered, diffuse, wandering | Curiosity, wonder, restlessness, whimsy | **Surge** — lightness diffuses a sudden, overwhelming intensity |
| **Surge** | Volatile, charged, sudden | Passion, anger, thrill, urgency | **Current** — sudden intensity overwhelms a gentle, flowing connection |
| **Current** | Flowing, adaptive, wave-like | Empathy, longing, nostalgia, compassion | **Hush** — connection reaches into and draws out someone closed off |
| **Hush** | Silent, still, internal | Grief, patience, quiet sorrow, solitude | **Bedrock** — patience and quiet endurance wear down even the most stubborn resolve |

Forms a clean five-type loop (each beats the one after it, loses to the one
before it): **Bedrock → Drift → Surge → Current → Hush → (back to Bedrock)**.

A sixth, non-wheel type exists — see **Harmonic** under [Unique attributes](#unique-attributes-harmonic).

(Names and exact gameplay leans/numbers are starting points — refine during
Phase 1 once combat design is on paper.)

## Companions — "Resonant Echoes"

Not animal companions (KHR's "Box Weapons"). Instead:

A wielder's most formative memory crystallizes into an Echo through **what it
*felt* like, not a measured frequency**. The power is generated by the
**emotional charge of the memory** — the vibration/waveform aesthetic is the
*visual cue* that charge produces, not the underlying mechanism. (This
distinction matters: it keeps the system emotionally grounded and relatable —
"my power comes from how I felt in that moment" — rather than reading as a
clinical measurement.)

That emotional charge manifests as a living **constellation-like structure**:
nodes connected by rippling waveform-lines, forming a silhouette that echoes
the memory itself (a person, a creature, an object, an emotion rendered in
geometric form).

Key properties:

- **The constellation is what the feeling looks like** — nodes are points
  where the emotion concentrates, the connecting lines are how it moves and
  ripples outward. The vibration/waveform visual is the *expression* of the
  feeling, keeping the visual language unified with "Strata Resonance" without
  making the feeling itself feel like a side effect of the visuals.
- **Grows structurally over time** — as the wielder's relationship to that
  memory deepens (or as they grow past / reconcile with it), the pattern gains
  nodes, sharpens, or reshapes. Visible, structural sense of progression.
- **Combat behavior reflects the memory's emotional charge** — e.g. resolve →
  offensive pressure, grief → drain/sustain, joy → support/healing, defiance →
  damage amplification. Ties combat kit design directly to character backstory,
  and naturally aligns an Echo's behavior with its wielder's attribute type
  (since both spring from the same emotional register — see the wheel above).
- **Summoning item**: a small carried object — working name **"resonance
  shard"** or **"tuning shard"** — that holds the memory and the feeling
  bound to it. Manifests the Echo once the bond is strong enough.

## Conduit — "Keepsakes"

Not signature rings (KHR's "Vongola Rings/Gear"). Instead:

A small personal item — pin, pendant, clasp, wrist-piece (flexible per
character, not locked to one form) — containing a mineral fragment calibrated
to the wielder's exact frequency. It's what makes raw Strata Resonance
*usable*: without one, a wielder's power is unstable and undirected (part of
why an unregistered awakening, like the protagonist's, draws attention — raw,
uncontrolled resonance is *noticeable*).

Key properties:

- **The Keepsake is the seed node of the Echo's constellation** — this is what
  makes the kit feel like one coherent system rather than two separate halves.
  The Keepsake is a single geometric node (the wielder's always-on resonance
  point); the Resonant Echo is the *fuller constellation* that
  eventually grows outward from that same node once the formative memory
  crystallizes. Conduit and companion share one visual language — geometric
  nodes and waveform-lines throughout — instead of KHR's disconnected
  ring-aesthetic / animal-aesthetic split.
- **Determines the wielder's attribute type** — the Keepsake is what turns raw
  resonance into *directed, typed* power (Bedrock, Drift, Surge, Current,
  Hush), the way KHR's Rings let a Guardian actually use their Flame in combat.
- **How it's obtained — no inheritance needed, but not guaranteed to be easy**:
  when Resonance first awakens in someone, a nearby mineral fragment ideally
  "answers" their frequency and becomes their Keepsake — organic, no bloodline
  or hand-me-down required (fits "competing factions, no single throne": no
  power passed down a lineage). **But that natural resonance doesn't always
  happen.** When a wielder can't bond with a fragment on their own, their
  power has to be channeled through **external means** instead — a
  faction-provided device, a ritual, a trained technique, someone else's
  guidance. This gives unanchored/struggling wielders a very concrete reason
  to seek out (or be sought out by) factions or mentors — directly setting up
  Salvatore's role with Suzu, and later with others (see [[character-roster]]).

## Unique attributes — "Harmonic" {#unique-attributes-harmonic}

Outside the five-type wheel sits a rare sixth attribute: **Harmonic** —
currently Salvatore's defining trait (see [[character-roster]]).

Where each standard type represents a single emotional *register* (resolve,
curiosity, passion, empathy, grief/patience), **Harmonic represents emotional
mastery — the ability to resonate in tune with all of them at once**, rather
than being anchored to just one.

- **Effective against all standard types**: can meet any emotional approach on
  its own terms and answer it — no register goes unrecognized or unanswered.
- **Resistant to all standard types**: can't be overwhelmed by a single
  emotional frequency, because none of them catch a Harmonic wielder off
  guard or "speak past" their understanding — all five are already integrated.
- **Framed as mastery, not a birthright** — reached through age, experience,
  and self-understanding, not something you simply start with. This leaves
  room for the cast themselves to grow toward something like it much later,
  turning Salvatore's standing from "a wall the player can't touch" into "a
  marker of where this could go."
- **Balance flag for Phase 1**: "beats and resists everything" needs care once
  Harmonic becomes an active combat factor rather than a story trait — the
  usual fix is offsetting costs (lower raw output, slower pace, situational
  limits) so it reads as *mastery* rather than *broken math*. Not deciding the
  numbers now — just flagging it so it isn't forgotten.

## Why this holds up as original

| KHR element | Our equivalent | What's different |
|---|---|---|
| Dying Will Flames (fire, passion) | Strata Resonance (vibration, harmony) | Different sense, different visual register, different emotional metaphor |
| Attribute wheel (weather-themed, no stated logic to the matchups) | Resonance States × emotional registers (Bedrock/Drift/Surge/Current/Hush) | Matchups read as emotionally *true* ("patience outlasts stubbornness"), not memorized trivia — and the wheel is literally built from the same "feelings generate power" idea the whole system rests on |
| Box Weapons (sealed boxes → animal companions, summoned from a measured "Flame" power) | Resonance shards → Resonant Echoes, crystallized from the *feeling* of a memory | Source (a feeling, not a frequency measurement or creature template), shape (geometric constellation, not animal), summoning vessel (shard, not box) |
| Vongola Rings/Gear (inherited, fixed form, separate aesthetic from Boxes) | Keepsakes (awakened organically *or* channeled externally when that fails, flexible form, share one visual language with Echoes) | Source (frequency-matched or externally-channeled, not inherited), relationship to companion (literal seed-node of the same constellation, not a separate system) |
| (no real equivalent — Flames don't have an "emotional mastery" tier) | Harmonic — a sixth, off-wheel attribute representing integration of all emotional registers | An original concept with no direct KHR analogue; framed as earned mastery rather than raw power tier |
